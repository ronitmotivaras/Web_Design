About Our Library

Welcome to Unity Library!

Welcome to Unity Library, a dynamic center for learning, research, and engagement. Our diverse collection includes fiction and non-fiction books, e-books, audiobooks, children's literature, periodicals, and special collections like rare books and local history archives. We strive to provide resources that cater to all interests and age groups, making our library a vital community hub for education and personal growth.

Our Collection

Books and E-books:

  • Children's Books: A dedicated space for young readers, with picture books, early readers, and young adult novels.
  • E-books and Audiobooks: Access our digital library from anywhere, anytime.

Periodicals:

  • Magazines and Journals:Stay updated with the latest in various fields such as science, technology, health, fashion, and current affairs.
  • Newspapers:Daily newspapers to keep you informed about local and global news.
Multimedia
  • Music CDs and Digital Music:A wide range of music genres available in both physical CDs and digital formats for streaming or download.
Community Services
  • Meeting Rooms and Study Spaces:Reservable meeting rooms and quiet study spaces for individual or group use.
Educational Programs
  • Workshops and Classes: Regular workshops and classes on various topics such as coding, digital literacy, and creative writing.
  • Tutoring and Homework Help:Personalized tutoring sessions and homework help for students of all ages.
Special Collections
  • Rare Books and Manuscripts: Access to rare books and special collections for scholarly research.
Accessibility Services
  • Assistive Technology: Tools and resources for patrons with disabilities, including screen readers, magnifiers, and accessible workstations.
  • Homebound Services: Delivery of library materials to homebound individuals who cannot visit the library in person.
